How do I add a new job application?

To add a new job application, go to the Applications page and click the "Add Job" button in the top right corner. Fill out the application form with details about the position, company, and application status.

How do I schedule an interview on the calendar?

Navigate to the Calendar page, click on the desired date, and use the "Add Interview/Event" form to schedule your interview. You can specify the time, type, and add any relevant notes.

How can I track my application status?

Your application status can be viewed and updated from the Applications page. Each application has a status indicator that can be changed as you progress through the hiring process.

How do I view my application analytics?

Visit the Analytics page to view detailed statistics about your job search, including application sources, response rates, and interview success rates.

Can I export my application data?

Yes! You can export your application data from the Analytics page using the export button in the top right corner.
